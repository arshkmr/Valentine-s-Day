One Platform to Design, Propose, and
Deliver AV Projects

Design faster. Draw smarter. Deliver complete AV docs.

X-DRAW is an AV-native drawing tool in XTEN-AV that lets you create line schematics, floor plans, rack layouts, and wiring diagrams in one place. It connects drawings directly to BOMs and documentation for a seamless AV workflow.

Features

Auto AV Documentation

Instantly create BOMs, cable schedules, and AV documents from designs.

Fast AV Rack Design

Plan and adjust AV racks and elevations in minutes, not hours.

Automatic AV Cable Labelling

Auto-label cables with consistent IDs and styles for clear AV diagrams.

AV Floor Plans & Layouts

Auto-import floor plans and quickly place AV devices, speakers and racks.

AV Schematics & Diagrams

Draw AV signal-flow and wiring diagrams with AV-specific tools.

3 Steps to Smarter AV Proposals with X-DRAW

Create & set up

Start from a template or past proposal, then pull in AV products, pricing, and client details.

Customize & refine
